About Us

At Pushp, we believe in creating a sustainable future through innovative solutions that benefit both people and the planet. Our mission is simple: to tackle pressing social and environmental issues by harnessing the power of nature and upcycling waste to create products that make a real difference. Born out of a passion for sustainability, Pushp is dedicated to addressing the problem of Sustainability and Reusability. , we believe in creating positive impact, and our solutions are rooted in community engagement and eco-conscious practices.

What We Do

At Pushp, we create innovative products by upcycling waste materials, transforming them into sustainable alternatives that make a real impact. Our products range from eco-friendly household items to gifting items, all made with care and purpose. We believe in the power of nature and aim to harness it by using waste materials that would otherwise end up in landfills, giving them a second life.

Join Us on Our Journey

Every product you purchase from Pushp directly supports our mission to create a better world. Together, we can contribute to a cleaner, more sustainable future—one product at a time.

Our Journey

What started as a small initiative with a dream to make a difference has now grown into a full-fledged enterprise with a clear vision for a greener future. It all began when we attended a hackathon, where we saw the potential of working together to solve real-world problems. During this experience, we were inspired by other brands that are already doing great things in the sustainability space. Their efforts showed us that change is possible, but we also realized there was room to add a little extra touch.

While many brands focus on sustainability, we wanted to go beyond just making products. We wanted to bring something unique to the table—solutions that don’t just solve problems but also engage communities and inspire action. It was clear that we could create something truly special by not just focusing on the product itself but also on the impact we could have on people and the planet.

Pushp is all about giving that extra push-whether it’s through upcycling waste in innovative ways, supporting local communities, or finding new solutions to old problems. We don’t just want to be part of the movement; we want to lead it with our commitment to creating a sustainable future that’s more inclusive, impactful, and conscious.
